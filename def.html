Defence

Defence Division of Lux & Decibels


Lux and Decibels is a technology-driven company committed to creating a combat training VR environment for our Country’s Bravehearts, specializing in the core technology such as: • Augmented Reality (AR), • Virtual Reality (VR), • Assisted Reality • Mixed Reality (MR) • X -Reality (XR)
X-Reality technology is the latest in the world. XR allows realistic multi-sensory experiences that create an unprecedented training experience. Lux & Decibels is based at Chennai, India and has a facility in Europe too. We have been in the technology field for over 2 decades and are in the forefront of technological advances. Thanks to our experience of working with other Indian defence establishments thereby understanding the ground reality.Based on our experience our primary goals are training of troops using real time weapons in a virtual world thereby reducing high costs of training in terms of spent ammunation, wear and tear of weapons and constant need for refresher training of troops to keep them in a battle-ready format.
Lux and Decibels wish to create a world-class virtual reality-based training program for the Indian Defence establishment. The program will help train troops in simulated war-like conditions in a virtual environment with a real feel of weapons and other dimensions via simulation. By use of Artificial intelligence, the troops would be able to see other men and enemies alike in the virtual world and we can create the following sensory conditions Temperature – Heat and Cold, Smell of propellent and explosives, Real sounds of firing weapons and other explosions, Real feel of the recoil of guns, Tanks, Artillery guns, etc.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) can simulate the heat generated inside a battle tank and real weather-based temperatures in the western borders, eastern sector or any environment. It’s been acknowledged that the cost of mock yet realistic combat training with real gear, weaponry, ammunition, and simulated conditions can run into a few lakhs or to even crores every time. Hence using our custom-designed training module will help impart the cutting-edge skills needed to react in a real battlefield scenario. We are confident that this independent training module with artificial intelligence will help bridge the gap in training and provide constant feedback matrix on battle preparedness.
